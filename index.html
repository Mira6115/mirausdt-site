About Mira USD (MrUSDT)

What is Mira USD?

Mira USD (MrUSDT) is an independent decentralized stablecoin pegged to the US Dollar, aiming to bring stability and reliability to the crypto ecosystem. Built on Binance Smart Chain (BSC), it offers fast, low-fee transactions and broad DeFi compatibility.

Note: Mira USD (MrUSDT) is not affiliated with Tether, USDT, or any official stablecoin provider. "USDT" in the project name refers only to value denomination.

Key Advantages

  • Lightning-fast BSC transactions
  • Open-source, audited smart contract
  • 1:1 US Dollar peg
  • Minimal fees, maximum accessibility

Why Choose MrUSDT?

Fast Transactions

Near-instant BSC transactions make MrUSDT perfect for daily crypto payments.

Stable Value

Always maintained at a 1:1 ratio with the US Dollar.

Wide Compatibility

Works with major wallets, exchanges, and DeFi apps (BEP-20 standard).
