About The Project

Our Mission

MiraUSD (MRUSDT) is a decentralized stablecoin built on Binance Smart Chain, designed to provide a low-fee, transparent, and community-owned stablecoin solution for the DeFi ecosystem.

Our vision is to create a stable digital currency that maintains price stability while being fully decentralized, enabling seamless transactions and financial operations across the blockchain space.

  • Fully decentralized stablecoin
  • Low transaction fees on BSC
  • Community governed
  • Transparent and audited
